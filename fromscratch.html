How do they form?

  1. The Sun Creates Charged Particles
    • The sun creates charged particles
    • Coronal mass ejections of plasma and magnetic field are emitted
    • High-energy radiation s
  2. Earth's Magnetic Field
    • The earth's magnetic field extends into the atmosphere and protects it from solar flares.
    • The solar flares are pushed to the polar regions of the earth.
    • The solar magnetic field colides with the earth's magnetic field in the atmosphere.
  3. Earth's Atmosphere: Different gases produce different colors when the magnetic fields collide.
    • Oxygen: Green or Red
    • Nitrogen: Blue or Purple or Pink
