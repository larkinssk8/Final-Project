About Samantha

I’m pursuing a path at the intersection of healthcare operations, speech-language pathology, and audiology. I’m passionate about rigorous research, autism advocacy, and practical hearing protection—especially empowering people whose communication differs from typical norms.

My work spans hospital continuous improvement, hands-on therapy support with autistic children, and qualitative research bridging neurotypical and autistic communication. I value evidence-based practice, empathy, and systems thinking.
